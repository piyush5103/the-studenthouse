About Us

Reeyan Udani started TeenagerWebNews (TWN) out as a means for him to provide information to people our age about things that are currently transpiring in our ever-changing world. However, as time has passed he decided to make his website a platform to inform and express for more than just himself. He hopes that this website can be an opportunity for students like him to portray their views and analysis on matters that they are passionate about. He has faith that in the future, this website will be a means in which students can enjoy learning about things happening on our planet and aspires that someday, this website can invoke the passion that he has, into its readers.

The Founder

Reeyan is someone who considers himself as someone who has a keen interest in international relations as well as global economics. That passion has led to him participating in over 15 Model UNs having received an award in all but one of them. Additionally, that fascination he possessed over politics and economics, is what inspired him to start his website in the first place.
