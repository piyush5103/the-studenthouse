SkilLAB

About Us

SkilLAB students differentiate themselves by creatively solving real world problems. They learn an array of transferable skills from critical thinking, research, analysis, and public speaking to implementing creative awareness campaigns and writing project reports.

The Founder

Devika Harlalka - Co-founder of SkilLAB has served as a peer mentor for 6+ years. She received her B.A. in Psychology from Emory University, USA and was awarded the ‘Emory Woman of Excellence’ honour for her outstanding work in social impact. She graduated ‘Alpha Epsilon Upsilon’, the highest academic merit and is pursuing her Masters in Education at Harvard University, USA.

At SkilLAB, Devika hosts Social Impact and Leadership summer programs where she mentors students in curating their social impact projects. These are aimed towards achieving the United Nations Development goals.

About the Program

SkilLAB Social Impact and Leadership Summer Program 2020

Curate your Social Impact Project that aims towards United Nations Developmental goals in a structured and mentored manner.

1 week Virtual program mentored by Devika Harlalka - Harvard Graduate School student and Co-founder of SkilLAB

Sessions:
• Welfare for Animals (June 15th - June 20th)
• Environment and Sustainability (June 22nd - June 27th)
• Malnutrition and Hunger (June 29th - July 4th)
• Poverty and Quality Education (July 6th - July 11th)
• Gender Equality and Women Empowerment (July 13th - July 18th)

Program Fees: ₹4,500
