  • Why The StudentHouse?

    The StudentHouse provides high/middle school students unique services that no other organization offers. If you’re struggling with managing your time because of the large quantity of work in the Checkpoints/IGCSE or IB, or you are struggling to balance your various extracurricular and school commitments, we have excellent mentors who are engaged in various extracurricular activities and still excel at academics. These mentors are ready to speak to you on a one-on-one basis and solve all your problems that you face as a student. Alternatively, if you’re confused on how to improve any of your skills, be they theatre, dance, debating or football, we have mentors who have mastered most of these skills while facing the challenges you will face over the next few years, and are ready to guide you on how you can enhance those skills. Furthermore, if you’re a student who would like to join an existing organization as a core team member/use the organization’s services/become a member in the organization we have a broad catalog of organizations that look for students across ages and have opportunities for all interests. This feature, known as Join a Project, will not only improve your extracurricular portfolio, but will also help you develop new experiences and meet new people. Finally, if you aim to create your own organization like Yohan, Raghav and Piyush (the Co-Founders of TSH), we would love to help you! Just use our Create a Project feature, attend our 7 personalised and interactive modules and kickstart your own project.

  • How much do I have to pay for The StudentHouse’s services?

    The StudentHouse is currently FREE. There is no added cost for any of our services. We are ready to help all students for FREE OF COST, as of now.

  • How do I become a Mentor?

    Currently, The StudentHouse is not recruiting any more mentors. However, this is not a lost opportunity! We will be opening up a second phase of mentor recruiting wherein we will be recruiting more mentors. So STAY TUNED for updates!

  • What will I be mentored about if I become a mentee?

    There are three kinds of mentorship a student may seek if he/she becomes a mentee. They can either be mentored in extracurriculars (learn more here), general student life (learn more here), or both! The mentor and mentee will have one-on-one meetings so that each mentee gets the time they deserve!

  • Will The StudentHouse hold any ownership in the projects they help create/affiliate with for 'Join a Project' or 'Create a Project'?

    The StudentHouse will not hold any ownership in any of the organizations we help create/affiliate with. We are solely being kick starters in the Create a project service and intermediates in the Join a Project service.

  • How do I affiliate with The StudentHouse?

    Use the Contact page and contact any one of the members on that page. We will get in touch with you from there on. It's that simple!
