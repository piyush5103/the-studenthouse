TheCandidSpeaker

About Us

CandidSpeaker aims to push back against the herd mentality with new and bold thinking and to provide a platform for otherwise unheard ideas, people, and places.

We are a group of young enthusiasts who aim to shine some light on the underrated and sumptuous news that needs to be appreciated more than it currently is. We found that most of the news is hiding among the intricate roots of our localities and we think it is time for this news to be heard. We feel the need to bring to light all the happenings of these localities on this one platform. We plan on giving out simple but very informative news to our readers.

How it will benefit you

We provide a platform for students to be aware about current situations and to share their thoughts on the topics that we put out. We aim on informing all our readers about the happenings around the world and to encourage them to do their part in being active by taking simple steps to be aware and act accordingly. We also provide a platform for students from various states to have an online debate on each of the topics we put out so that we understand each other's views on that particular topic.
