Create a Project

1

Meet with our founders to ideate and formulate your project

You can meet us (Raghav and Yohan) personally and have a discussion about the starting phase of the project. We can discuss preliminary ideas, brainstorm concepts, and help you with the initial conception of your project. We will help you take the first step in transforming an idea into a well executed, tangible product.

2

Inform the team leaders about your project idea

We will put you in touch with our communications team who can act as liaisons between you and our organisation. You can get to know the leaders of our marketing, communications, events and illustration teams who can have an initial conversation with you to discuss the current stage of your idea.

3

The leader will set up a meeting with you and the team

You can meet each of the aforementioned teams individually, and engage in detailed discussions about the specific aspects of your idea and where these teams can help you. Here, you can take advantage of the scale of our teams to get new and innovative ideas.

4

Complete the simple tasks given with the help of the teams

(example: working on the website marketing campaigns)
Our teams will give you tasks and objectives for you to not only build your project but, in doing so, gain the experience required to enhance and fortify your understanding of the core idea you are conceptualising.

5

Resolve any other doubts you have for your project or ask for additional help if needed

Re-evaluate your project and gain insights and knowledge to clarify any questions and concerns you may have after completing the tasks and objectives at this stage in the development process. Use this as an opportunity to extract the most out of our valuable and experienced team

6

Finalise your project idea after taking all the inputs

Finish preparing the small details of your project and use everything that you have learned from the above stages to generate a plan and task list which will carry you through the execution of this idea.

7

Kickstart your project!

Launch your project and take advantage of our website platform to immediately spread the word about it. Follow your plan and use the resources available on our website to ensure that you can take the initial, small idea and transform it into a thriving startup initiative or project!
