About Us

ClickedMedia, is a full service digital marketing agency, curating affordable digital experiences that transform any brand’s visibility. The aim of ClickedMedia is to enhance a brand's presence by maximising digital visibility and enforcing long-term consumer relationships. It is a one stop solution for all your digital marketing needs from Social Media Marketing, Content Creation and Website Marketing, I have all the tricks up my sleeve to position a brand relevantly, and have gathered professionals to work with me to ensure that each of our clients have a digital strategy that does creative wonders in enhancing their brand presence. This pandemic has badly affected all small businesses and has also forced business activity to go online, which is why ClickedMedia provides a very affordable outlet for small businesses to effectively market their business online and as a result gain organic clients.
Our services include

  • Social Media Marketing
    Giving people the complete package, we design Instagram grids, put out well written captions, research for the most effective hashtags, work on the most captivating stories and more importantly, engage with the target audience of that brand.
  • Content Writing
    We efficiently intertwine content that resonates with the brand's identity with stylistic pieces of writing that amp up their online presence.
  • Website Marketing
    Offering SEO Services, Email Marketing and Google Ads services, we ensure qualified traffic to any website, through the help of professionals.

The Founder

My name is Ryka Mehra, and I just finished tenth grade at BD Somani Intl, I'm an avid dancer, public speaking enthusiast and since the 9th grade have also undertaken a keen interest towards learning business. That soon developed into a niche for marketing. I have interned for digital marketing agencies, worked as a freelancer social media manager and have also completed certified courses to ensure I could learn the most about this field. After noticing that small businesses were suffering economically in this ever-growing digital age, I decided to launch an affordable outlet that doesn't stunt digital growth even if economic growth has slowed down.
