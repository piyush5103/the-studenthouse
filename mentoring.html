Extra-curriculars

Need to build your college application? Struggling to find an extracurricular activity which aligns with your interests? Perhaps you want to start your own individual project, and need help either getting it off the ground or marketing it effectively. The StudentHouse is here to help! Simply create a new project, or join an existing one. Using the create a project tab, you can even merge an existing project onto our platform and take advantage of our powerful marketing tools.

General Student Life

As international school students, we are always inundated with work and commitments. Sometimes, we find ourselves confused, lost, and in need of guidance. How does one balance all of one’s commitments, academic or otherwise, effectively? How can an individual master the elusive art of time management? Look no further than our mentorship program, which allows students to be connected to seniors from across the city for guidance and advice.
