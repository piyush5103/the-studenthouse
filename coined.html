CoinEd Bulletin

About Us

CoinEd Bulletin is more than just a social media news providing platform. The word news is overused and is a general term used for providing information, however, today's news is simply the reporting of facts or opinionated arguments. Hence, We at CoinEd believe in the idiom: just as there are two sides to a coin there are two sides to every story. Thus, we are providing you the opportunity to voice your opinions that conflict with others in a documented method unlike ever before. Bringing you unfiltered, unbiased and reliable information with two sides to every argument to truly signify objective journalism.

The Founders

Jash Shah & Aryan Shah

Jash Shah & Aryan Shah

CoinEd Bulletin was founded by Jash Shah (11th Grade) and Aryan Shah (12th grade). We started this organization due to our inclination towards global affairs and our active involvement in MUN. Apart from our shared interests in global affairs, we also do share a passion for understanding global economics and this project is just the perfect way for us to combine our interests and redefine the way information is seen.
