MMDU

About Us

MMDU is an online platform that helps students attain the education of their dreams. We do this by connecting hardworking high school students to students at top universities around the world. These university students mentor a small group of high school students in one on one Skype sessions where they guide them through the application process, cultivation of an extra-curricular profile, understanding the diverse educational systems in universities and countries and much more. Our Brilliant Mentors guide their mentee through every facet of their high school and university education, as seasoned veterans of this journey who have succeeded in reaching the most elite universities in the world, they’re advice revolutionises student’s educational prospects and reality.

Services
Student Mentorship
Our High School Student Users speak to their mentors in one on one online sessions a minimum of once every month where they set academic, extra-curricular, internship, sport and other type of goals and targets. During our sessions, Mentees enjoy the company of a student at one of the world’s most prestigious universities and converse with them to cultivate their application and student profile. Our Mentors, are the brightest minds at the best universities and the benefits of being mentored by them are intangible.

The Founder

Hi! My Name is Sumeir Chhabria and I am a 11th grade student at The Jayshree Periwal International School. I am a seasoned public speaker, actor, MUNner, polyglot, CISVer and entrepreneur who is determined to provide students with the guidance and knowledge they need to attain the education of their dreams. MMDU is my first venture toward my vision of making high school students more aware of and prepared for higher education. I am immensely proud of my team and the impact we have had on the 700 students that have used our platform despite having launched only 20 days ago. I hope that MMDU can impact your educational journey and I look forward to working with you!

How it will benefit you
  • Academic Enrichment and Engagement Planning : Plan your standardised testing, AP Examinations, High School Curricula (IB, AP, OSSD etc..) along with competitive and specialised examinations with the most experienced and intelligent students who have excelled in these academic areas.
  • Resume Building : Our Mentors will help you cultivate your resume by helping you demonstrate passion, interest and excellence in the arts, sports and the plethora of potential extra-curricular you can excel at. They will also shed light onto the importance of internships, demonstrated interest and various other aspects of the resume building process.
  • Application Process Aid : The best way to learn about the UCAS/Common Application Process is from someone who has been through it and been very successful at it! Our Mentors will guide you through every technical aspect off your application while also helping you create a compelling and differentiating student profile.
  • Much Much More! It's impossible to describe the impact an MMDU mentorship programme has on students. The learnings are intangible and foster an inquisitive and active mindset, that drives success and growth.
