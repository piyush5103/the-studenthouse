About Us

Established in the year 2020, CONQUER is an organisation/company that provides a platform for the youth to vanquish their dreams. We, at CONQUER bolster the rising athletes and provide them a platform to showcase their talents with an ulterior motive of propagating the development of sports in India. You may address what makes CONQUER unique and that is the fact that we target the lesser promoted sports in India, like Hockey which is our national sport but is given little to no importance, and through CONQUER we plan to have a sole intention behind each event we host giving the lesser known sports a chance to gain the spotlight and spread awareness about the same.

Vision - WE UNITE. WE CONQUER.

Mission - To propagate the power of sport by uniting the youth.

Aims and Objectives

  • Engendering the improvement of sports in India from it's grassroots.
  • Providing a platform not only for recreation, but also with equivalent opportunities for the present youth to feature their abilities.
  • Primarily targeting lesser promoted and played sports in India for example: Throwball, Hockey etc.

The Founders

Yahna Mehta, one of the founding members of CONQUER, is a talented 17 year old, currently studying at B.D. Somani International School. She graduated from The J.B. Petit High School for Girls in the year 2019. She plays Football for her school and Throwball at several levels. Having captained and played for the Indian Women's Throwball team, she aspires to propagate the development of sports in India.

Ali Sharafally, one of the founding members of CONQUER, is an ambitious 17 year old, currently studying at Jai Hind College. He graduated from Campion School in the year 2019. Having played competitive football since the time he could walk, as well as being an aspiring lawyer, Ali strives to drive the establishment of a law specifically in the field of sports in India.

How it will benefit you

This was mainly for promoting the development of sports and the best thing any student can have on their resume or a part of their extra curricular activities would be being a part of an initiative that is spreading awareness about the same. Secondly, there are many students who strive to excel in their field of sport and this would be a platform that provides them scope for individual development and recognition for the sport they play.

Note from the Founders:
Greetings from the CONQUER family, We, as Founders being extremely attached and associated with several sports believe that there should be a platform for every individual out there who aspires to become an athlete and wishes to conquer their dream. The entire concept of CONQUER was born with merely a dream which led us to planning an online gym battle. But what great could just a gym battle do, hence we thought of this very frequently used but powerful word-'CONQUER'. Championing young and aspiring athletes and uniting them by giving them a chance to engender the advancement of sports in India.The sole purpose of creating this organization was to create awareness and change the way Sports as a whole works in India. In recent years, there has been an improvement from the recognition of several sports whereas some still remain unheard of. Through this organization we want to put forward the message of the power of sports and work towards its improvement and development in the future.
