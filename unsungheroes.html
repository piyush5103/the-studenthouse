Unsung Heroes

About Us

The Unsung Hero’s Team is a group of 8 young students who have taken the initiative to collectively spread awareness about the various unsung heroes of India. Every member of the team contributes to their specific role. With our own creativity and unique talent, the Unsung Heroes Team aims to promote the unhonoured individuals of the disadvantaged Indian society. With multiple articles, collaborations and interviews with other organizations, the team has successfully impacted and inspired several others to help contribute in raising awareness about the unsung heroes of India. The Unsung Heroes Team’s primary objective is to positively impact our community in order to develop an appreciation and respect for the unacknowledged individuals of India. As our motto suggests, the Unsung Heroes Team will continuously create ‘stories that inspire’ and honour the overlooked social workers of India.
Our Services
In order to raise awareness about certain individuals, our team provides opportunities to people, inspiring them to contribute to our team’s initiation.

  • Article Writing
    Sign up to write articles about your appropriately chosen unsung hero, based on our article writing guidelines. These articles would help our team create ‘stories that inspire’ and promote the unsung heroes of India.
  • Interviews
    Contribute by providing a verbal appreciation through an interview with another organisation/team of your choice. They must be involved in a social initiative that positively impacts society. Such interviews would also help us promote other organisations’s initiatives and persuade others to contribute with various initiatives. The interviews will be published along with an interview report that is written by a writer.

How it will benefit you

By contributing to our initiation, students will benefit from gaining knowledge about the unknown heroes of India. Moreover, the opportunities and services that we provide will allow students to discover their skills and talents in the process of impacting the community. Not only will participating help spread awareness for our and any other similar organisation, but it will also positively influence and inspire students overall. By stepping up as individuals and promoting various individuals in their own manner, students are involved in making a difference in the several communities of India.

The privileged and advantaged community are the ones who can positively influence, inspire and greatly impact the lives of multiple unsung heroes. This is beneficial to the disadvantaged society as several individuals would gain their well-deserved recognition. This simultaneously impacts various underprivileged communities as many more individuals would aspire to gain the title of an unsung hero. In addition to this, the reputation of such communities would be improved overall as well.
