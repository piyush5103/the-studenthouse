Join a Project

About it

Trying to find an existing extracurricular which appeals to you and your interests? Using our “Join a Project” feature, you can glance through existing projects which have been set up by other students, allowing you to jump right in! Take a look at other projects and initiatives on this page, started for students and by students. You can click on any of the projects on this page to get more info or get in touch with the founders of the project. The time and oft resource consuming tasks of ideating, setting up a project, laying its foundation and creating its core functions, and designing a website, have all been completed by the creators of the projects on this page. This means that all that’s left for you is just to get involved and immediately reap the rewards of initiatives whose seeds were sown months before and have matured into a stable and accessible project. Keep in mind that our catalogue will continue expanding. For more information on starting your own project, view the “Create a Project” page.
